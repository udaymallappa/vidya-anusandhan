About

To alleviate the rising costs associated with quality JEE coaching, Vidya Anusandhan Foundation (VAF) was set up by Rahul Chovva and Uday Sharma. Rahul was a student of Delta (2006 - AIR 3499) and a graduate of IIT-Kanpur. Uday was a student of Delta (2006 - AIR 4425) and a graduate of BITS-Pilani. Both Rahul and Uday have excelled in their academic pursuits and careers, and have now chosen to repay their guru dakshina to Delta.

Who can apply

Delta students who are pursuing their 11th and 12th are eligible to apply for the scholarship. The decision to grant the scholarship is decided based on a written exam conducted at Delta. After successful grant of the scholarship, VAF shall pay up to 50% of the student's academic fee.

How to apply

Interested students are required to write an exam, conducted by Delta. For more details on the scholarship and the exam, please contact +91 6301438660 or visit www.deltaiit.com.
